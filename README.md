# 🛡️ Shield Insurance Analytics Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview
This project focuses on building an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze business performance for an insurance company.

The dashboard provides insights into revenue trends, customer growth, sales channel performance, and demographic behavior.



## 🎯 Objectives
- Track total revenue and total customers
- Monitor monthly and daily performance trends
- Analyze performance by city, age group, and sales mode
- Enable interactive filtering for business decision-making



## 📌 Dashboard Pages

### 1️⃣ Executive Overview
- KPIs: Revenue, Customers, MoM Growth
- Avg Daily Revenue (DRG)
- Avg Daily Customers (DCG)
- Revenue by City
- Trend Analysis (Toggle)

### 2️⃣ Sales Overview
- Revenue by Sales Mode
- Customer Distribution
- Sales Mode Trend Over Time

### 3️⃣ Age Group Analysis
- Customers by Age Group
- Revenue by Age Group
- Policy Preference Matrix
- Settlement Analysis



## 📈 Key Insights
- Offline Agents contribute ~55% of total revenue
- 31–40 age group is the largest customer segment
- Revenue peaks in March (seasonality trend)
- Digital channels show strong growth potential



## 🛠️ Tools & Skills Used
